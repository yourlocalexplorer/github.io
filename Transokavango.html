The Trans-Okavango Charity Cycle Team

FOLLOW ON FACEBOOK

If you want to go fast go alone and if you want to far then go together. This proverb comes to mind when describing the journey experience by the Trans Okavango Charity Cycle team. During the year of 2020 any form of positivity is valued but when four esteemed gentlemen who love tourism come together to give back to the community in a fun manner, then the world must take a bow and appreciate..

“We are a group of tourism professionals that have been working in the tourism industry for the last 20 years and have partnered with a local charity organization - Naletsana Charity Organisation which is focused on rural communities in and around the Okavango Delta and Ngamiland with the aim to transform the lives of those in the communities offering them hope for the future and livelihood.

The proposed plan is to do an awareness Bicycle tour from Seronga Village to Maun. We want to support the local communities that are directly affected by lack of tourism due to COVID-19. The people from these communities are now home without jobs.

We already have some local companies and individuals that have donated or helping in kind. Our wish is to continue supporting this course and your support would push us an extra mile on this quest.”
