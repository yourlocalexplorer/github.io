6 Things to do in Gaborone during the coming holidays

If you'll be in Gaborone during the holiday here are a couple of activities you could do.

1. Cinema

Are you a lover of big screens and popcorn? Do you wish to get that big thematic sound of the movies you watch? Then I guess New Capital Cinema is the place for you. It’s up to date with the latest movies, plus great a place to take a girl for a romantic movie date. Located in three separate places: Riverwalk mall, Masa complex and Gamecity mall, you have the comfort of avoiding crowding and watching your movie with less noise from those loud dramatic movie watchers. 

2. Go-carts

I have a driver’s license and sometimes I wish I could get the most out of my driving. You know what I’m talking about ha-ha, speeding, drifting and the euphoria of being number 1. So I avoid traffic violations and head to the western side of Ditshupo hall, to get the thrill of things. At an affordable price P100-150, I get to do 4-6 laps around the track, burn rudder, skid around tight corners and yes look at the sad face of my buddy as I pass them to cross the finish line as number 1. I’m not competitive but I believe sometimes we have to brag a little about some things. If I am speaking to you then go-carts should be in your to do list. 

3. Hill climbing


Kgale hill is the best place to practice your climbing skills. With its unique location it provides a great view of the surrounding area. You even get to see air traffic as they head to SSKB and be one with the pilots. Plus, as you get higher, you leave the noise of traffic down below and get a more serene feel up top.

4. Restaurant


What is a weekend without good food? So many restaurants around gabs and so many cuisines to choose, from Setswana all the way to Chinese cuisine all your taste buds will be filled. For a classier fine dine you can visit Table50Two located at Itowers in the CBD and enjoy your weekend in class. 

5. Parks

Picnics, well-kept gardens, the sound of kids playing on swings, you can experience all this at FNB park. It’s such a beautiful place to be with the family. It’s well secured so don’t worry about losing your possession, as a matter of fact bring your large towel and pillow and go sleep under a tree. 

6. Video Gamming
Alternate Universe located in Gamecity is the place to be if you want to play a FIFA tournament with your buddies. There is a wide list of games to choose from and big screens to play on. What more is to be said it’s a gamers paradise.
